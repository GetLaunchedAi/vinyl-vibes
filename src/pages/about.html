About Us

Who We Are

Vinyl Vibes is a record store lounge & bar located in Mt. Clemens, MI. We combine a curated collection of vinyl records with craft cocktails, creating a space where music and community come together. Our selection features everything from timeless classics to fresh new releases, along with unique merchandise from local artists and small businesses.

More than just a bar, Vinyl Vibes is a social hub for music lovers — a place to connect, relax, and celebrate underrepresented voices in music and art. Whether you’re browsing records, sipping cocktails, or hosting an event, we bring the vibes every night.

"Our mission is simple: celebrate music, uplift community, and create a vibe you won’t find anywhere else."
