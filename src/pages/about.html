Who We Are

At Bright & Brown Solutions LLC, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality residential and commercial cleaning services across Michigan. Whether it’s basic or deep cleaning, carpet shampooing, floor stripping and waxing, window cleaning, or even room painting — we make sure every project is done right the first time.

Our founder studied janitorial trade and horticulture, building a strong foundation in cleanliness, attention to detail, and pride in hard work. That passion shines through every service we provide — ensuring homes, offices, and facilities not only look their best but also feel fresh and welcoming.

"A clean space is more than just appearance — it’s peace of mind. We work thoroughly so you can enjoy a spotless, worry-free environment."

Ralph Brown Founder
